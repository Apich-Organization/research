# Biography: Apich Organization Science & Research Team

The **Apich Organization Science & Research Team** is an independent unit that evolved from the organization's core consulting branch. Composed of dedicated and independent researchers, the team specializes in **PINN (Physics-Informed Neural Networks), AI4S (AI for Science), and HPC (High-Performance Computing)**, with extended expertise spanning cybersecurity and theoretical physics.

## Collaboration & Open Source Integration

While entirely autonomous, the Science & Research Team works in close synergy with the **Open Source Development Team**. We provide innovative architectural concepts and, in turn, leverage the robust projects maintained by the open-source community. This cycle often drives our research: code conceptualized within our team frequently matures into next-generation open-source software, which is then handed over to the maintenance team for long-term stewardship.

## Compliance & Contact

The Science & Research Team strictly adheres to all **Apich Organization Security Team standards**, as well as international academic and dual-use research frameworks.
